About the Institute

Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT), Noida was established in the year 2001 and has been constantly ranked among the top engineering Institutes in Delhi NCR. JIIT has been accredited and approved by AICTE, NIRF, MHRD and has continued to become a centre of excellence in emerging areas of science and technology.

Vision

To become a centre of excellence in the field of IT and related emerging areas of education, training and research comparable to the best in the world for producing professionals who shall be leaders in innovation, entrepreneurship, creativity and management.

Mission

IstMission

To develop as a benchmark University in emerging technologies.

2nd Mission

To provide state-of-the-art teaching learning process and R&D environment.

3rd Mission

To harness human capital for sustainable competitive edge and social relevance.

Mission Of ICABB 2023

IstMission

To offer contemporary, futuristic and flexible curricula of Biotechnology for teaching and training.

2nd Mission

To carry out globally acceptable cutting edge research through sponsored projects and to provide state of art laboratories for experimental work.

3rd Mission

To develop bio safe, socially ethically and environmentally acceptable solutions to address health, environmental, industrial, entrepreneurial and societal concerns.
